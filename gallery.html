About Us

Who are the Bandicaps?

The Bandicaps Golf Society epitomizes the perfect blend of leisure and camaraderie for golf enthusiasts. Amidst stunning landscapes, this society embraces a laid-back approach to the game, focusing on the social aspect as much as the golfing itself. Members gather for relaxed rounds where laughter and shared stories are as important as the strokes.

With a diverse membership ranging from beginners to seasoned players, everyone finds a welcoming environment to play, connect, and unwind. Through their regular friendly matches and post-game gatherings, the Sunshine Social Golf Society encapsulates the true spirit of golf as a means of building connections and cherished memories.
